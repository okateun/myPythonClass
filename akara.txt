# Akara Recipe

This is how we make akara.

What is akara?
Akara is a tasty Nigerian delicacy made from black-eyed beans. It is known by different names around the country.

How is it made?
Black-eyed beans is soacked for hours until the beans peels off through gentle coninuous scrubs or rubs. When this process
is completed, the beans is washed and strained. Salt, pepper, bell-pepper, onions, are some of the ingredients added and
blended to have a nice mixture. The final stage, is fyring, until brown, not burnt or blackened.

Also be safe when frying, do not use olive oil for best result. Fry at a low temperature!!
